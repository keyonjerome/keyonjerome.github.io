FRC VISION

For the past two years in the FIRST Robotics Competition, I’ve worked on vision tracking (computer vision, using cameras) for our team’s robots. My programs, running on embedded systems such as the Jetson TX1 and Raspberry Pi, use video cameras and lights to detect field targets in real-time.

My most recent FRC Computer Vision project used Azure Machine Learning. I submitted it to the BOS Raspberry Pi Contest, where I came in first place and won $2,500 in sponsorships for my team this year.

The technologies I’ve used in these projects include Tensorflow, CustomVision.AI, OpenCV, NetworkTables and more.
