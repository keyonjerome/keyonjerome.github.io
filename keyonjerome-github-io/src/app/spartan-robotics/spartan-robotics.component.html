SPARTAN ROBOTICS

This isn’t exactly a project—but by all means, it’s my largest commitment yet.

As captain of my school's FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team since September 2018, I've organized meetings, presentations, led robot programming, and led business activities for our team. We build a ~100-lb robot each year.

This year alone, I've raised $6,000 in sponsorships from companies in the London area by organizing presentations.

More information about our team is available at spartanrobotics.ca.
